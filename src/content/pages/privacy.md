## The Basics

At 5Y Holdings Pte. Ltd. (5.Y), we respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with global privacy regulations, including GDPR and PDPA.

## What We Collect

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tools to enhance your experience and analyze website traffic. Cookies are small text files stored on your device, allowing us to understand visitor behavior and improve our services.

### Personal Data

If you contact us through forms, we may collect details like your name, email address, and message to respond to your inquiries.

## How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely for:

- Enhancing website performance and improving user experience.
- Responding to inquiries or fulfilling requests submitted via the website.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties unless required by law.

## Opt-Out of Tracking

You can manage cookies and other tracking methods via the cookie setting on your first visit to the website, or via your browser settings. To opt out of website analytics tracking, such as Google Analytics, you may use the Google Analytics Opt-Out browser add-on.

## Your Rights

As part of our commitment to privacy, you have the right to:

- Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
- Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

## Data Security and Retention

We implement security measures to protect your information and retain your data only for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes outlined above.

## Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

For any questions or concerns about your data, please contact us at [dpo@five-whys.com](mailto:dpo@five-whys.com).
